# stream
1. Log into the ward laptop:
* Username: Greenoaks
* Password: "Greenoaks1234!" (no quotes)
2. Launch OBS Studio first (icon should be on the desktop)
3. Verify camera connection - you should immediately see a video feed from the chapel's camera. If not, use the remote to turn the camera on.
4. Open Chrome (must be Chrome, not another browser)
5. Navigate to the Greenoaks Ward Member tools site. There should be a link on the Chrome bookmark bar. The address is https://greenoaks.wardtools.org/
6. Click "Stream Controller" from the left navigation. You can access the left nav by clicking the hamburger menu icon in the top left area of the page.
7. When you are ready to stream, navigate to OBS Studio and click the link in the lower right-hand corner of the application that says something like "Manage Broadcast and Start Stream"
8. In the new window, choose the option to begin streaming (lower right of the window?)
9. The stream is managed through the Ward Tools website. Camera views are controlled by icons along the top of the program.
10. Hymns are updated for today's program. Click the hymn block for the appropriate hymn and your camera angle will change and the hymn lyrics will begin to appear on the stream. You will need to click each lyric block manually as the music progresses.
11. When the hymn is finished, click the "Close" button in the lower right-hand corner of the page.
12. When ready for the Sacrament, click the "Administration of the Sacrament" block to enable the OBS overlay.
13. When the Sacrament is complete, click the "blank" block - it will reset your view.
14. When the stream is complete, click the "End Stream" button from Ward Tools or the "End Stream" button in OBS Studio.
